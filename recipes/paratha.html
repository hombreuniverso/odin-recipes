Paratha

Paratha with Curried Gravy

Paratha or buss up shut is a favourite to cook during social events and family reunions. This recipe serves 10 persons with a set up time of about 25 minutes. The overall cooking time is is approximately 1 1/2 hours.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of flour
  • 6 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 teaspoons of bakng powder
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 3/4 cups of water

Directions

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, salt and water
  2. Knead to a soft dough and make four balls. Leave for 15 - 30 mins
  3. Roll out the dough
  4. Spread butter and sprinkle with flour
  5. Cut from centre to edge and roll into a cone
  6. Press the peak and flatten the centre of the cone. Leave for 15 - 30 mins
  7. Roll out on a flat surface
  8. Coat the preheated tawah with butter
  9. Place the flattened dough on the tawah
  10. Spread the butter on one side then turn the other side to cook
  11. Spread butter on the other side the same way
  12. When both sides are cooked, break up the roti with a dabla
  13. Serve with curried chicken and potato gravy
