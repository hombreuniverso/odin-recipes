Pelau Decorated with Fresh Salad

Pelau Delight is a quick and easy meal to prepare when you are in a hurry to wip up something fast and delicious. It is one of my favourite foods. This recipe serves 10 persons with a set up time of about 5 minutes. The overall cooking time is is approximately half and hour.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 10 small pieces of chicken
  • 2 cups of canned pigeon peas
  • 4 cups of rice
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 3 leaves of thyme
  • salt
  • 1 pack of coconut milk

Directions

  1. Place sugar in pot and heat until liquid dark-brown
  2. Add pigeon peas to pot and stir
  3. Add chicken to put and stir
  4. Add water until ingredients are submerged
  5. Boil until meat is soft and tender
  6. Add rice and seasonings
  7. Add salt and butter to taste and allow water to evaporate on a low flame
  8. Turn off flame when rice is properly cooked
  9. Serve with fresh salad
