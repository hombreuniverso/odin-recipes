Savory Assorted Vegetable Soup

Lentil and carrot soup is quick and easy to cook. This recipe serves 7 persons. The preparation time is 5 minutes and the overall cooking time is 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 5 glasses of water
  • 2 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 4 carrots coarsely grated
  • 140g of lentils
  • pepper
  • salt
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 2 leaves of thyme
  • salt
  • 1 pack of coconut milk

Directions

  1. Place 5 glasses of water in a pot
  2. Add all the ingredients except salt
  3. Cook until lentils and carrots are soft and tender
  4. Add salt to taste
